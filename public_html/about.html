About Us

Dotty’s Tvet

Unlock the boundless world of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEAM) with us. We are dedicated to providing innovative and engaging educational experiences that inspire young minds, foster creativity, and cultivate a passion for learning. At Dotty’s Tvet Institute, we believe that every child has the potential to become a future innovator, problem-solver, and leader. Our carefully designed programs and activities cater to children aged 5-16, offering a wide range of exciting opportunities to explore the fascinating realms of coding, woodworking and joinery, science experiments, robotics, and strategic thinking. Dotty’s TVET Institute, our aim is not only to make profit, but also to give children a solid foundation for future security and stability.
